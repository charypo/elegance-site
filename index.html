About Us

About Us

Nontrivial Solutions delivers cost-effective, subscription-based software (SaaS) platforms tailored to the complex needs of small and medium-sized organizations. Our over 30 clients value our savvy, agile, and partner-focused approach, trusting us with their most critical operations.

We thrive on solving intricate challenges, creating solutions that are both practical and powerful. Our two flagship products are fully online and accessible from both desktop and mobile devices: ChartCaddy, a secure online medical records system for outpatient health providers, and NativeManage, a centralized platform for Native organizations to manage data, programs, and services.

Nontrivial Solutions builds upon more than 30 years of experience in software development and project leadership from its founder, James Bearden. A proud member of the Sault Ste. Marie Tribe of Chippewa Indians, James embodies a career defined by turning complex projects into measurable success.

ChartCaddy

ChartCaddy

For outpatient health organizations that need simplicity without compromise. ChartCaddy makes medical records easy to manage, share, and secure. All your records are in one place and flow seamlessly—giving your staff more time for care, not paperwork.

Key benefits:
  • HIPAA-compliant — secure, cloud-based records
  • 100% digital — eliminates paper forms
  • Accessible anywhere — on any desktop or mobile device
  • Highly customizable — aligns to each client’s unique workflows
NativeManage

NativeManage

A modern platform built with Native organizations, for Native organizations. NativeManage brings together member, service, and program records into one secure digital system.

Key benefits:
  • Eliminates spreadsheets — and paper forms; keeps all records in one place
  • Customizable — aligned with each tribe’s unique administrative operations
  • Accessible anywhere — desktop or mobile, supporting leaders and staff on the go
  • Scalable — for small nations with growing needs
